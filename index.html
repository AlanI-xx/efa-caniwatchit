Australian Piracy

Are the top 10 most pirated movies available for legal digital rental or purchase in Australia?


We know that Australian theatrical, DVD and VOD releases lag behind the rest of the world by as much as months. Australians aren't reluctant to pay for content - a Pocketbook study found that despite the fact that it is actively geoblocked in Australia, Nexflix is the second most popular paid-content media company in Australia.

With a desire to keep up with the rest of the world and a willingness to pay for it, are there legal digital alternatives to movie piracy in Australia?

Methodology


First, we take the top 10 most pirated movies on BitTorrent as reported by TorrentFreak each week.

Then, we check the availability of each film in Australia on the following services:

  • iTunes
  • Google Play
  • Quickflix
  • BigPond Movies
  • Viewster
  • Crackle
  • Presto
  • EzFlixTV

We have deliberately discounted services like Fetch TV that require special hardware like topboxes or gaming devices. We've also had to discount services like Mubi and Foxtel Play because you must be a member to search their selection.
